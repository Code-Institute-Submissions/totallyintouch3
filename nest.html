The Nest Learning Thermostat learns the temperature you like when you’re at home. It turns itself down when you’re out to help save energy. And you can control it from your phone.Just turn it up and down. With Auto-Schedule, the Nest Learning Thermostat learns from you and programs itself. Change the temperature from anywhere using your phone, tablet or laptop.Use your home assistant and your voice to change the temperature. The Nest thermostat starts at £219.99 plus installation
