This is LG's Laundry family which consists of their latest flayship Twinwash system. TWINWash™ is a washing solution that has never existed before. It is all about efficiency, helping you get more clothes cleaner in less time by washing two separate loads simultaneously. TWINWash™ Mini, a pedestal type washing machine, helps give special care to your loads of laundry.

The LG SIGNATURE washing machine is loaded with SmartThinQ™ technology. You can operate the washing machine and receive smartphone notifications when the detergent tank is empty, download specialised wash cycles and even track the amount of water and electricity used. .

InstaView Door-in-Door™ has a sleek mirrored glass panel that illuminates with two quick knocks, allowing you to see inside the easy access compartment without ever opening the door, reducing cold air loss to help keep food fresher for longer. Use the sleek controls or your smartphone to monitor and control your refrigerator with ease. Auto Open Door feature is a smart sensor detector at the foot of the refrigerator that opens the door automatically. Just step on the "Door Open" light projection on the floor in front of the refrigerator and the door gently opens.

With the Melitta® Connect App many of the features on your fully automatic coffee machine can be managed with your smart phone. For example, appliance settings can be managed conveniently and personal My Coffee Profiles can be programmed and saved. Practical tutorials assist you with cleaning and maintaining the appliance. A special highlight with this app is that you can create your very own coffee specialities.
